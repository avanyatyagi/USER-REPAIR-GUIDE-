Product: Laptop

Description: A laptop computer is a portable personal computer powered by a battery or an external power supply.

Repair Instructions

  1. Turn off the laptop and unplug it from the power source.
  2. Remove the battery if possible.
  3. Locate the screws securing the bottom cover and remove them using a screwdriver.
  4. Carefully pry open the bottom cover using a plastic opening tool.
  5. Identify the component causing the issue (e.g., hard drive, RAM, etc.).
  6. Replace or repair the faulty component as needed.
  7. Reassemble the laptop by following the steps in reverse order.
  8. Test the laptop to ensure the issue has been resolved.
